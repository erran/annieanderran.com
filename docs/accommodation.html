Accommodation

You can choose to stay at Galgorm, with its crashing rapids, complimentry award winning thermal village and spa, or you can also stay close by.


Staying at the Galgorm

If you choose to stay at the venue you'll get a wedding guest rate by mentioning Carey and Sheridan when booking. You can check out the Venue's website here to view various types of rooms.

You must book through phone (number can be found on the Galgorm's website) or via email.

To give an idea of pricing this is what Galgorm has given to us as room prices including discount.

However, you are not limited to choose one of the below rooms. There are Cottages and other rooms available, so while browsing the Galgorm website if you see a room you would like, you can phone or email to book. Make sure to quote Carey and Sheridan for discount!
